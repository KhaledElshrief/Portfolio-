Mechanism:-

>application begins with an onboarding process that guides users through an initial onboarding screen before directing them to a login screen. For new users without an account, they are directed to a registration screen where registration rules for email format and password requirements are strictly enforced. Upon successful validation, the app communicates with a PHP backend via the Dio package to securely store user data on the server. >in home screen users can browse a diverse range of products such as clothing and electronics directly from the home screen. Each product listing offers detailed information including price, available colors, and a list of pros and cons. Users can seamlessly navigate to a dedicated screen for each product to explore comprehensive details before making a purchasing decision.
